Effective date: January 1, 2025. This policy explains how Yemeni Coffee Co., doing business as Shibam Coffee (Shibam Coffee Atlanta, "we," "us"), collects and uses information through this website.

When you submit the contact or catering form on this site, we collect the information you provide — name, email, phone number, and any event details or message you include. We do not require an account or collect payment information through this website.

Analytics & Advertising Cookies

This site uses Google Tag Manager to load analytics and advertising tools, including Google Analytics (GA4), Meta Pixel, and TikTok Pixel. You can control cookies through your browser settings, and most browsers let you block or delete them.

How We Use Information

We use the information submitted through our forms to respond to catering and event inquiries, answer general questions, and follow up about your visit. We do not sell your personal information.

Form submissions are processed through a third-party form service. Analytics and advertising data is processed by Google, Meta, and TikTok under their respective privacy policies.
